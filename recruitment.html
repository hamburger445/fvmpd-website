Recruitment information

Career progression is earned through time, training, and conduct, not favors. Titles below match our Discord rank structure.

Ranks

High Command

  • Chief of Police
  • Deputy Chief
  • Assistant Chief

Administration

  • Human Resources / High Ranking (--FVMPD HR --)

Supervision

  • Captain
  • Lieutenant
  • Sergeant
  • FVMPD Supervisors (-- FVMPD SUPERVISOR --)

Enlisted

  • Senior Officer
  • Officer
  • Probationary Officer
  • Recruit

General role

  • FVMPD Low Ranking (-- FVMPD LR --)

Promotion criteria are posted internally. Ask recruitment for the current matrix.

Divisions and units

  • Patrol: primary response, traffic, and preventive patrol in the AORP (for example Greenville).
  • Investigations / CIU-style units: longer-form cases when the department opens billets.
  • Training: FTOs and instructors who run academies and in-service blocks.

Benefits of joining

  • Promotions: clear path from probation to leadership for members who invest.
  • Activity rewards: commendations and events that recognize consistent presence.
  • Skills: radio discipline, report writing, and de-escalation practice you can use in other RP groups.
  • Community: teammates who care about lore, fairness, and improving every class of recruits.
