Join / Apply

Ready to wear the badge? Start here. We look for maturity, reliability, and willingness to learn, not perfection on day one.

Before you start, read the application information posted in the FVMPD Discord. It will help you a lot.

Open application (Google Form)

Fox Valley Metro Police Department Application: traffic stops, pursuits, and traffic management scenarios. Answer honestly. There are no word-count requirements, but longer, thoughtful answers stand out. Inaccurate or joke answers may be denied.

Requirements

  • Age: Must be 13+ (Discord Terms of Service). We also expect maturity in how you carry yourself.
  • Activity: Ability to patrol or attend training on a predictable basis. Tell us if you will be away.
  • Behavior: No recent serious disciplinary history. Be respectful in Discord and in-game. Follow the chain of command.
  • Voice and text: Readable English in reports and radio-style comms.
  • Character fit: Willingness to play within department lore and uniform/vehicle guidelines.

What to expect after applying

  1. Review: recruitment or command reads your application.
  2. Interview or scenario: short conversation and/or practical exercise (traffic stop, radio use).
  3. Academy / field training: structured blocks covering policy, workflow, and ride-along evaluation.
  4. Probationary period: supervised patrol with feedback before full status.

Timelines vary by class size. Stay active in Discord so you do not miss pings.
