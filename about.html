About Fox Valley Metro Police Department

FVMPD models a modern suburban police department: responsive patrol, accountable supervision, and standards that match real-world policing values adapted for our roleplay community.

Mission

Protect life and property, enforce the law fairly, and build trust through professional conduct, clear communication, and consistent follow-through on calls and investigations.

Jurisdiction (AORP)

Our primary area of roleplay patrol (AORP) centers on Greenville and surrounding routes. Officers are expected to know major corridors, common call locations, and how to coordinate with allied agencies when scenes overlap.

Exact map boundaries and partner jurisdictions are announced on Discord when command updates them.
